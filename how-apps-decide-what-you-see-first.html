How Popular Apps Decide What You See First

Popular apps rank content based on user behavior rather than randomness. Each interaction helps shape what appears in your feed.

Main Ranking Signals

  • Watch time and dwell time
  • Likes, comments, and shares
  • Content relevance
  • User interaction history

Understanding these signals helps users and creators better navigate app feeds.
